Other Stats

Most Finishes (All-Time)

Ranked by total number of successful finishes across all years including 2015. Unlike the Lucky Tribers list, this includes participants who have also DNF'd.

Longest Consecutive Entry Streaks

Ranked by the longest unbroken run of entries (every year the race was held). Finishes and DNFs are counted within that streak window.

Most Different Boats Used

Tribers who have successfully finished on the most variety of different boat models. DNF entries don't count.

Most Different Classes Raced

Tribers who have successfully finished in the most different classes (C1–C6, CS). Only finishes count — DNFs don't. Max anyone has achieved is 4 classes.
