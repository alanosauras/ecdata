Other Stats

Most Finishes (All-Time): Ranked by total finishes — EC finishes plus UFC Stage 1 completions (which cover the same route as the EC). Unlike the Lucky Tribers list, this includes participants who have also DNF'd.

Longest Consecutive Entry Streaks: Ranked by the longest unbroken run of entries (every year the race was held). UFC entries count — showing up on the beach for Stage 1 is the same start line. Finishes and DNFs are counted within that streak window.

Most Different Boats Used: Tribers who have successfully finished on the most variety of different boat models. DNF entries don't count.

Most Different Classes Raced: Tribers who have successfully finished in the most different classes (C1–C6, CS). Only finishes count — DNFs don't. Max anyone has achieved is 4 classes.
