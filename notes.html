Cp1 Change:

After 2012, the location of CP1 changed to Cape Haze Marina and no longer required class 4 boats to un-step their masts to get under a low bridge to get to the original CP1 at Grand Tours. This change led to the use of much larger boats and also reduced the length of the course slightly.
