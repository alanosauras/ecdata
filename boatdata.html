Below are all unique boat types in order of their number of finshes. Did not starts are not included in attempts.

In an attempt to rate the boats from the data a "weighted score" is calculated using a 50/50 weighted average of the number of unique crews for each boat as well as it's success rate. This highlights boats that have a good success rate accross multiple unique crews rather than just one hotshot crew that use the same boat every year. When Sorting by this weighted score, boats with less than 2 unique crews are not shown since data with that few points has little meaning.
